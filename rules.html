Rules & Regulations
01

Students should be dine only in their enrolled mess. No student is allowed to enter any other mess and is also not allowed to take her friend from other mess to her mess.

06

Every month students are allowed to change their mess through their Student Login by following the instructions given in the circular sent to them during the last week of the month.

02

Students enrolled in FOOD PARK are allowed to order and take food only to a maximum of Rs. 400/- per day. Students should note that FOOD PARK is also mess and not a restaurant.

07

Carrying of plates, glasses, spoons, etc., from the mess is strictly prohibited and if found is punishable.

03

Unwell Students can get their request approved by the warden for food to be delivered to their room.

08

All diners should interact with the mess staff in a courteous manner and maintain the decorum of the mess.

04

Wastage of food is strictly prohibited.

09

Students are not allowed to carry their meals out of the mess and likewise from one mess to the other to dine with their friends.

05

Mess and Menu committee meetings are held once every month to finalize on the menu for the next month and to receive suggestions from the mess coordinators and students dining in the mess.

10

Students are advised to register any mess related issues in the Suggestion Note Books kept in every mess.
